Presentation

Don't you have allready search about a movie based by movies you really love?

Don't you have allready search about a movie based by your preferences?

Stop it! MovieNetwork is here to help you about these searches.

The principle is really simple: you really like a movie, and wanna see an other which look like at this one. No problem! Put the name of your favorite film in the input, and our algorithms will output all the movies which seems to like you.

MovieNetwork contains a big database of movies. Also, if you don't find the movie you like the most, you can upload a small form about it!
